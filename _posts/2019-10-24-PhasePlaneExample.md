I decided to make a short (well, 16-min. video) on how to
- Write down a set of equations for an ecological interaction (competition, here)
- Find nullclines (for 1 species, but you always want to do both)
- Map nullclines onto a phase plane (species 1 and 2 on the axes)
- Identify equilibria
- Determine stability of equilibira

For each ecological interaction type, there should be corresponding numbers of different outcomes:

| Interaction  | Number of outcomes |
|--------------|:------------------:|
| Neutralism   | 1                  |
| Mutualism    | 2                  |
| Competition  | 3 (or 4)           |
| Enemy-victim | 2 (or 3)           |
| Commensalism | 1                  |
| Amensalism   | 2 (or 3)           |
