This weekend a paper I wrote with some collaborators was published online (i.e., not just with the journal's final issue/volume and page numbers). The paper is called *Fast and slow advances toward a deeper integration of theory and empiricism*. It's not a technical or experimental paper, but rather somewhat of a historical commentary. The journal *Theoretical Ecology* is looking to start a series of articles that "revisit the classics." These papers are usually a nice refresher of contextualizing highly-influential papers. We were asked to write the second part of this series for the journal. We revisited a paper on insect outbreaks from the 1970s. It was a fun project and I'm proud of our final product.

Have a gander if interested: [link](https://link.springer.com/article/10.1007/s12080-019-00441-x)
